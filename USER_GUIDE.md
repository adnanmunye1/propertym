## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Open your web browser and navigate to PropertyM
2. Enter your **email** and **password**
3. Click **Sign In**

**Note:** Contact your administrator if you don't have login credentials.

## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard provides a quick overview of your property management operations.

### Key Metrics

- **Total Properties**: Number of properties you manage
- **Total Units**: Total units across all properties
- **Rent This Month**: Rent collected vs rent due
- **Arrears**: Outstanding balance from tenants

### Arrears Alerts

If tenants have overdue payments, you'll see a red alert box showing:
- Tenant name
- Property and unit
- Amount overdue
- Days overdue

**Action:** Click on a tenant to view details and payment history.

### Quick Actions

Three convenient shortcuts:
- **Add Property**: Register a new building
- **Add Tenant**: Register a new tenant
- **Record Payment**: Log a rent payment

## Managing Properties

### Viewing Properties

1. Click **Properties** in the sidebar
2. View list of all your properties
3. See property type, city, and number of units

### Adding a New Property

1. Click **Properties** ‚Üí **Add New Property**
2. Fill in the property details:
   - **Name**: Property name (e.g., "Sunrise Apartments")
   - **Address**: Street address
   - **City**: City name
   - **County**: County name
   - **Property Type**: Apartment, House, Commercial, or Mixed Use
   - **Total Units**: Number of units
   - **Description**: Additional details (optional)
3. Click **Save Property**

### Editing a Property

1. Go to **Properties**
2. Click on the property you want to edit
3. Click **Edit Property**
4. Update the details
5. Click **Save Changes**

### Deleting a Property

**Warning:** You can only delete properties with no units.

1. Go to property details
2. Click **Delete Property**
3. Confirm deletion

## Managing Units

### Viewing Units

1. Click **Units** in the sidebar
2. View all units across all properties
3. Filter by:
   - Property
   - Status (Vacant, Occupied, Maintenance)

### Adding a New Unit

1. Click **Units** ‚Üí **Add New Unit**
2. Fill in unit details:
   - **Property**: Select the property
   - **Unit Number**: Unit identifier (e.g., "A1", "101")
   - **Bedrooms**: Number of bedrooms
   - **Bathrooms**: Number of bathrooms
   - **Square Feet**: Unit size (optional)
   - **Rent Amount**: Monthly rent in KES
   - **Deposit Amount**: Security deposit
   - **Status**: Vacant, Occupied, or Maintenance
   - **Description**: Additional details
3. Click **Save Unit**

### Viewing Unit Details

1. Click on a unit to view:
   - Unit specifications
   - Current tenant (if occupied)
   - Rental history
   - Maintenance status

### Editing a Unit

1. Go to unit details
2. Click **Edit Unit**
3. Update details
4. Click **Save Changes**

### Deleting a Unit

**Warning:** You can only delete vacant units with no tenancy history.

1. Go to unit details
2. Click **Delete Unit**
3. Confirm deletion

## Managing Tenants

### Viewing Tenants

1. Click **Tenants** in the sidebar
2. View all tenants (active and inactive)
3. Use search to find specific tenants

### Adding a New Tenant

1. Click **Tenants** ‚Üí **Add New Tenant**
2. Fill in tenant details:
   - **First Name**
   - **Last Name**
   - **Email**
   - **Phone**: Include country code (+254)
   - **ID Number**: National ID or passport
   - **Emergency Contact**: Phone number
   - **Occupation** (optional)
   - **Employer** (optional)
3. Click **Save Tenant**

### Assigning Tenant to Unit (Move-In)

1. Go to tenant details
2. Click **Assign to Unit**
3. Select:
   - **Unit**: Choose vacant unit
   - **Start Date**: Lease start date
   - **End Date**: Lease end date (if fixed term)
   - **Rent Amount**: Monthly rent
   - **Deposit Amount**: Security deposit paid
   - **Rent Due Day**: Day of month (1-31)
   - **Lease Term**: Fixed or Month-to-Month
4. Click **Create Tenancy**

**Note:** The unit status will automatically change to "Occupied"

### Moving Out a Tenant

1. Go to tenant details
2. Find the active tenancy
3. Click **Move Out**
4. Enter:
   - **Move Out Date**
   - **Final Balance**: Any outstanding or refundable amount
5. Click **Complete Move Out**

**Note:** The unit status will automatically change to "Vacant"

### Viewing Tenant Details

Tenant details page shows:
- Personal information
- Current tenancy
- Payment history
- Outstanding balance
- Documents
- Invoices

## Recording Payments

### Recording a Payment

1. Click **Payments** ‚Üí **Record Payment**
2. Fill in payment details:
   - **Tenant**: Select tenant
   - **Amount**: Payment amount in KES
   - **Payment Date**: Date received
   - **Payment Method**: M-Pesa, Bank Transfer, Cash, or Cheque
   - **Reference**: M-Pesa code or cheque number
   - **Notes**: Additional information (optional)
3. Click **Record Payment**

**Note:** Payment automatically reduces the tenant's balance.

### Viewing Payment History

1. Click **Payments** in the sidebar
2. View all payments
3. Filter by:
   - Tenant
   - Date range
   - Payment method

### Payment Details

Click on a payment to view:
- Tenant information
- Payment amount
- Payment method
- Reference number
- Date and time
- Notes

### Deleting a Payment

**Use with caution!**

1. Go to payment details
2. Click **Delete Payment**
3. Confirm deletion

**Note:** This will adjust the tenant's balance.

## Viewing Reports

### Payment Report

Track all payments over a specific period.

1. Click **Reports** in the sidebar
2. Select **Payment Report**
3. Choose date range
4. Click **Generate Report**
5. Export as CSV or Excel

**Shows:**
- All payments received
- Payment methods
- Total collected
- Tenant details

### Arrears Report

View all tenants with outstanding balances.

1. Click **Reports**
2. Select **Arrears Report**
3. Click **Generate Report**
4. Export if needed

**Shows:**
- Tenant name
- Unit and property
- Balance owed
- Days overdue
- Last payment date

### Occupancy Report

Track unit occupancy and vacancy rates.

1. Click **Reports**
2. Select **Occupancy Report**
3. Click **Generate Report**

**Shows:**
- Total units
- Occupied units
- Vacant units
- Occupancy rate
- Units in maintenance

## Managing Documents

### Uploading Documents

1. Click **Documents** in the sidebar
2. Click **Upload Document**
3. Select file from your computer
4. Choose document type:
   - Lease Agreement
   - ID Copy
   - Proof of Income
   - Other
5. Associate with:
   - Tenant (optional)
   - Property (optional)
6. Add description
7. Click **Upload**

**Supported Formats:**
- PDF, JPG, PNG, DOCX
- Max size: 10MB

### Viewing Documents

1. Click **Documents**
2. Filter by tenant or property
3. Click on document to preview

### Downloading Documents

1. Go to document details
2. Click **Download**

### Deleting Documents

1. Go to document details
2. Click **Delete Document**
3. Confirm deletion

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I delete a payment by mistake?**  
A: Yes, but you'll need to confirm. The tenant's balance will be adjusted accordingly.

**Q: What happens when I move out a tenant?**  
A: The tenancy is marked as ended, the unit becomes vacant, and the final balance is recorded.

**Q: Can one tenant occupy multiple units?**  
A: Yes, you can assign the same tenant to multiple units.

**Q: How do I handle partial payments?**  
A: Record the actual amount received. The system will track the remaining balance.

**Q: What if rent increases?**  
A: Edit the tenancy to update the rent amount, or create a new tenancy when the lease renews.

**Q: Can I print reports?**  
A: Yes, export reports to Excel or CSV, then print from your spreadsheet software.

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

- Check your email and password
- Ensure Caps Lock is off
- Contact your administrator to reset password

### Can't See My Properties

- Ensure you're logged in
- Check your user role permissions
- Refresh the page

### Payment Not Showing

- Wait a few seconds and refresh
- Check if payment was successfully recorded
- Verify the tenant was correctly selected

### Document Won't Upload

- Check file size (max 10MB)
- Verify file format (PDF, JPG, PNG, DOCX)
- Try a different browser
